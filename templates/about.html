About MedDetect

Revolutionizing medicine identification and information access through AI technology

Our Mission

MedDetect aims to bridge the gap between medicine identification and comprehensive information access. We believe that everyone should have easy access to accurate, reliable information about their medications to make informed healthcare decisions.

Our AI-powered platform combines cutting-edge image recognition technology with a comprehensive medicine database to provide instant, accurate information in multiple languages.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for medicine identification and information, serving millions of users worldwide with accurate, multilingual, and comprehensive medicine data.

We envision a world where medicine safety and information accessibility are not barriers to proper healthcare, regardless of language or location.
